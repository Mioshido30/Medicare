About Us

Founded in 2021. Medicare started as a small family-owned health care store in Kemanggisan, Jakarta. As more and more people get to know about Medicare, it is natural for us to expand out stores to other cities and province. Now, we have more than 100 stores spread out in the every major cities in indeonesia, including but not limited to Aceh, Medan, Palembang ,Jakarta, Surabaya, Pontianak, Balikpapan, dan Makassar. We also offer a wider variety for products from healthcare, cosmetics, medicines, and many more. We have a commitment to say true to our humble origin and serving our customers with best customer services and high quality products.
